The Festival of Sacrifice

Eid ul Adha commemorates Prophet Ibrahim's willingness to sacrifice his son as an act of obedience to Allah. It marks the end of the annual Hajj pilgrimage to Mecca.

Eid ul Adha Mubarak!

May Allah accept your sacrifices and shower you with His blessings on this special day.
